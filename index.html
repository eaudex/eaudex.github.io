All Things Optimized

Hi there! My name is Guoxun Yuan. I am a software engineer with recommender systems and search relevance as focus. When I was at school, my research was on optimization algorithms for large-scale classification and regression. Moving on to the industry, I mainly focus on applied machine learning, more specifically, applying machine learning and AI technologies to optimize web page content and product recommendations for marketing use cases. As a fan of big data technologies, I like to play with large data sets with Hadoop and Spark. If you also like any of these, we may find something interesting to chat about.
